Human–computer Interaction

Sidong Feng

PhD, Monash University

I am a PhD student in the Faculty of Information Technology at Monash University (Monash), working with Dr. Chunyang Chen. My research interests lie at the intersection of Software Engineering, Human-Computer Interaction, and Deep Learning. I received my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from the Australian National University (ANU) in Australia, advised by Prof. Zhenchang Xing. My current research focus is software testing and Human-AI interaction including the following three directions.

  • UI Design [CSCW'2019, CSCW'2020]
  • UI Implementation [IUI'2021, TIIS'2022]
  • UI/App Testing [ESEC/FSE'2020, ICSE'2022, ESEC/FSE'2022, ICSE'2023, ICSE'2023 (Distinguished), ICSE'2024]
