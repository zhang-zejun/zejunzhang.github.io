About me

I am a research student at Australian National University, Australia. My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, Deep Learning and Software Engineering. I obtained my B.S from School of Computer Science and Engineering, Australian National University (ANU), Australia, under the supervision of Zhenchang Xing and Chunyang Chen.

In addition to conducting better research, I have extensively collaborated with other outstanding researchers on various fields such as Computer Vision, Natural language processing etc. and from various countries such as Australia, Singapore, China, etc.

I am currently preparing for my Doctor of Philosophy (Phd) at Monash under supervision of Chunyang Chen. And I am keeping looking for industrial experience. Download my CV (English) and CV (Chinese) here.
